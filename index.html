    Goals

    1. To Digitalise Computer Labs :


    As in current scenarios, teachers conduct labs and give programs to be performed. A small bunch of students usually performs those activities and take print for the entire batch. We provide a better solution for this by proposing a Hackerrank like platform for each student, where teacher will create lab manual, set their own test cases on which students will perform using Online Compilers. Here teachers do not have to go on every desk in order to validate students' code.

    2. To provide Quick Quizzes:


    Currently teachers make google form for quizzes doing copy-paste for each questions and answers which is a troublesome job and takes time. Here we provide teachers the power of webscrapping using which question and options will be pulled in one go and will make this task quick and easy.

    3. To provide a better zone of Discussion:


    Students have Pre Lecture, In Lecture, Post Lecture doubts but don’t get a better platform to ask, in class of 60 sometimes it is not addressed as well. And also only a single person handling 60 requests is tough. So we provide a better platform where students can ask/post their doubts which will be addressed by anyone in class who knows be it a teacher or student.

    4. Webinars at one place:


    Due to the pandemic webinars are picking up pace.But the problem lies is the each webinar information and the zoom link is scattered in a cluttered whatsapp group.These messages are often missed resulting in loss for the students and the intention to keep the wenbinars.As a result this system has all the webinars at one go one click enroll.No messy messages anymore

    5. Complete analysis of a student's past performance:


    Considering this lockdown conditions as well, marks for current semester were calculated based on students past performance. This ‘past’ term is very important to assess performance of any entity and that's why it should be maintained properly. For this we provide a complete dashboard where students as well as teachers can view a students performance on any casual basis. This performance will be evaluated based on their first to last year scores.
